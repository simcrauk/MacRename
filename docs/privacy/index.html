MacRename Privacy Policy

Short version: MacRename does not collect anything.

What we collect

Nothing. MacRename has no analytics, no crash reporting, no telemetry, and no user accounts.

Network access

MacRename does not make any network connections. The app is fully offline. It does not phone home, check for updates, sync to a cloud service, or contact any server we control or any third party.

Files on your Mac

MacRename reads and renames files and folders that you explicitly drag into the window, pick through the Open panel, or send to it via Finder's right-click menu. Filenames, paths, and image metadata (EXIF / XMP) read from those files are used solely to compute the rename preview shown to you, and never leave your computer.

The app runs in the macOS App Sandbox and only ever touches the files and folders you have granted it access to during the current session.

Settings

The following preferences are stored locally in macOS user defaults, scoped to MacRename's own container:

  • Your last search and replace terms (so they are pre-filled when you reopen the app)
  • A list of recently used search and replace terms for the quick-pick menu
  • Toggle states from the Options panel

These never leave your Mac. You can clear them by deleting the app's preferences via defaults delete com.macrename.app in Terminal, or by removing the app entirely.

Children

MacRename is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the new version will appear at this URL. Material changes will also be noted in the app's release notes.

Contact

Questions or concerns about privacy can be sent to the maintainer via the project's GitHub issues page.
